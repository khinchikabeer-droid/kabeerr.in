Scissors: A useful tool in everyday life

Scissors is one of the most commonly used equipment used in our daily life. From cutting papers to using it in hospitals it is one of the most important equipment in our lives. Form classrooms to home it plays an important role in one’s life.

What are scissors?

It is a tool made up of two sharp steel blades which are attached to a common hinged point. It also consists of handles which when are pressed, moves the blade and cut the material placed between them.

Types of scissors

People are moved a far way and made enhancement in almost every daily use things. There are different types of scissors used for different purposes:

  • Paper scissors – It is used by students for their art work and cutting paper.
  • Fabric scissors – This type of scissors are used by tailors for cutting clothes and stitching clothes.
  • Kitchen scissors – used to cut food items.
  • Safety scissors – Used by children with round edges.

Uses of scissors

  • Used in schools by teachers to teach students art work.
  • Helpful for tailors for stitching clothes.
  • Used in offices for cutting paper.
  • Used at home in daily tasks.

Importance of scissors

Scissors make cutting work easier and helps in doing work neatly. It saves our time and effort and also helpful in creative activities like drawing, designing, and crafting. Learning to use scissors is also an art and it also improves hand coordination and concentration.

Conclusion

Scissors yet a simple tool but very important in our life. It makes our life tasks easy and to be done efficiently. Proper use of scissors and using it with care make them long lasting and keep us safe as well.

The importance of a sharpner in our daily life.

A sharpner is a tiny tool but an important stationery item. It is used to sharpening a pencil which is needed and used by students, teachers, artists and professionals. Though it is short and simple but plays a pivotal role in making our handwriting better and clear.

What is a sharpner?

It is a device used to make pencil grip sharp. It usually consist of a blade made of metal fixed inside a plastic body with a hole in it. When pencil is placed inside the hole and is rotated, the blade removes the wooden part and sharpens the graphite tip.

Types of sharpners

There are many types of sharpners commonly used for sharpening pencils.

  • Manual sharpners- It is small in size and are easy to carry, commonly used by students.
  • Electric sharpners- There types of sharpners are run by electricity and quickly sharpens the pencil.
  • Box sharpner- It has a box which stores the pencil shavings after sharpening.

Uses of sharpners

  • Holds the grip of the pencil and makes the writing good.
  • Makes sketches and drawings easier.
  • Saves time during exams.
  • Maintains the shape of pencil.

Importance of sharpner

It is an important tool to maintain a pencil tip grip which ensures that are handwriting is neat and improves accuracy. Without a sharpner a pencil becomes dull and blunt which eventually makes it difficult to use. It also teaches students about cleanliness when using box sharpners.

Conclusion

As we all know about small things hold a big impact or importance in our daily lives, a pencil also helps us keep our pencil ready to use and improves our work quality. A sharpner truly proves that small things holds a big change in society.

Eraser: A small tool with a big role

Among all the stationery items eraser is something which is always being ignored and sidelined. However it is important in our academic learning. It helps us correct mistakes and improve accuracy.

What is an eraser?

Ab eraser is a stationery tool used to remove pencil marks from paper. It is usually made from rubber, vinyl, or plastic. On using it marks of graphite made up of carbon are removed and makes the surface clean and ready to use again.

History of the eraser

In olden times until it’s not discovered bread crumbs are used to erase pencil marks. In the 18th century, rubber was discovered to be effective for erasing graphite. This led to invention of the eraser, which later evolved into different shapes, sizes, and materials.

Types of Erasers

There are many types of Erasers available today

  • Rubber Erasers – Commonly used in schools.
  • Vinyl or plastic erasers – Clean erasing with less smudging.
  • Kneaded erasers – Used by artists for shading and detailing.
  • Mechanical erasers – Pen like erasers for precise work.

Importance of an eraser

An eraser whether it is neglected but is an important tool for students to learn from their mistakes. It faiths practice without the fear of permanent errors. For artists and designers, erasers are essential tools for refining drawings and adding precision.

Eraser in daily life

Beyond academics, As said by some great peoples every small thing teaches us something big, that mistakes are not permanent. Just like pencil marks, errors can be corrected with effort and patience.

Conclusion

Whether eraser is small in size it plays a big role in education and creativity. It teaches us that if we learn something new mistakes are normal and can be rectified. It is natural and part of growth. The eraser is truly a silent helper in every students journey.

Classmate Registers

In every classroom, there is one object that silently observes everything without saying a word. This object is none other than the class register. It is on the teacher’s table. It looks very common and uninteresting. However, it contains the names of all the classmates and also bears the memories of a whole year. It appears to be nothing more than a journal with a list of names and numbers. However, it is much more than that for students.

Every morning, when the teacher opens the register and begins to call the names, the class comes to life. When a student responds to the call of a name with “present, ma’am” or “yes, sir,” this sounds simple. However, it signifies the presence of an individual with a identity and a role in the class. Some names are always first, and others last. Gradually, the children become aware of their identity in the register.

It also mirrors the diversities of fellow classmates. It contains various names, cultures, and personalities. Behind each name, a person who laughs boisterously, a person who keeps quiet, a person who assists others, and a person who struggles along silently. Nevertheless, there are times when the register evokes mixed feelings. When a name is called and there is no reaction, the teacher waits and marks the register accordingly. The blank reaction comes with a weighted presence, especially if the fellow student has been absent for a few days. Sometimes it may be an indication of illness, at other times it may be an indication of problems at home, and sometimes it may indicate the departure of a student from the learning institution.

However, on happier days, the register is associated with happiness. Prior to exams, trips, or celebrations, the roll call feels easier. Students react quicker, sometimes smiling or giggling. During birthdays, when the teacher unwittingly call the birthday child’s name, the classroom responds in claps and joking. The register unwittingly participates in the celebrations.

And as the year goes by, the register is filled with dates, signatures, and attendance records. And slowly, the register holds a page full of not only dates but the passage of time as well. Children grow, friendships evolve, memories are forged, but all along, the register records.

Parker Pen: Timeless symbol of elegance and excellence

History

The Parker pen company was found in 1888 by GEORGE SAFFORD PARKER in United States. His moto was that “It will always be possible to make a better pen.” When pens often get leaked and causes inconvenience parker focuses on designing pens that were reliable, smooth and comfortable to use. One of uts advancef development was ink feeding systems that reduced leakage.. This innovation quickly make parker popular among professionals, writers and students.

Innovation and design

Parker pens are known for their technological innovation and elegant design. Over the years the company introduced many features such as the lucky curve ink system which prevents leakage of pens. Later innovations include smooth nibs, refillable cartridges, and durable materials. Parker pens are the class of style and aesthetics. Whether it’s any type for eg. Fountain pen, ball point, rollerball, or gel pen, parker products reflect refinement and precision.

Popular parker pen models

Some parker pen models have achieved legendary class. The Parker 51 pen launched in 1940s, is considered one of the best fountain pen ever made due to its sleek design and reliability. The Parker jotter, introduced in 1954 became global favourite for everyday use, especially among students and professionals. Even today, parker jotter remains one of the most widely used pens in the world. The more luxurious premium collections like Parker sonnet, Parker duofold, and Parker IM cater to users who value luxury. These pens are often chosen as gifts and symbols of achievement.

Parker pen as a status symbol

Parker pens are more than writing tools but also status class and symbols of success professionalism. All the important documents treaties and agreements have been signed using parker pens because it shows class and based on their premium quality they are used. It also increases reputation because they are often gifted on special occasions such as graduation,promotions and anniversaries. Owing this pen increases attention to detail and appreciation.. It represents confidence, authority, and elegance.

Parker in modern times

Despite rise in relevant technology parker pen maintains its class. The pen focuses on sustainability by producing refillable pens reducing plastic waste, and promoting long term use instead of disposable writing tools.
